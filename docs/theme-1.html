Theme 1 - Social Interaction

Successful conversations can be really rewarding. The lessons in this module will help you give a positive first impression, buy the perfect gift, and resolve a difficult issue in English.

Lesson 1 - Meeting People

The topic of this lesson is meeting new people and describing their or your own character. You will listen to a conversation between people meeting each other for the first time and read emails about personal information. This will help you to be able to talk about a person's character.

  • Mario's profile

Pronunciation

THEME 6 - PRESENTATIONS AND NETWORKING

Introdução

"The world of work is all about giving presentations and making connections with people. These tasks can be a little less intimidating if you have the right language at your fingertips. In this module, you will learn a variety of functional language to help you with good presentations and practise expressions for arranging events, organising travel and maintaining good work relationships. You will better be able to discuss future projects, give feedback and write project reports."
